All New Tecno Camon 30

Information

The Tecno Camon 30 is a modern smartphone that combines performance, an elegant design, and advanced features.

Display

It features a 6.78-inch AMOLED display with a resolution of 1080 x 2436 pixels, providing an immersive visual experience and a 120Hz refresh rate for smooth navigation.

Under the hood

the Camon 30 is powered by a MediaTek Helio G99 processor, supported by 8GB of RAM and 256GB of internal storage, ensuring efficient performance for daily applications and multitasking.

Camera

For photography enthusiasts, it offers a dual rear camera setup with a 50MP main sensor and a 2MP secondary sensor, along with a 50MP front camera, ideal for detailed selfies.

Battery

The 5000mAh battery supports 70W fast charging, ensuring long-lasting usage with reduced charging times.

Design

The design of the Camon 30 stands out with a nature-inspired texture, providing a comfortable grip and a refined aesthetic. The phone is available in several colors, including Basaltic Dark Island, Uyuni White Salt, and Sahara Sand Brown.
